Our Story

The Beginning

The Day We Met

It still amazes me the way we met, two strangers on an app, unaware that one simple conversation would turn into something so deep and meaningful. Those first few months weren't just about knowing you, they were about feeling something I didn't even realize I was missing. Every message from you became the best part of my day, and I found myself waiting for you without even noticing it. Our late night conversations became my comfort, and somehow, despite the distance, you felt closer than anyone around me. What made it even more special was how you were there through every part of your day, talking to me while you were on your way to work, in between your busy office hours, and again when you were heading back home. And even after all those conversations, it still felt like I could never have enough of you, like no matter how much we talked, my heart always wanted more..

The Transitional Phase

First "I Miss You"

Somewhere between the fourth and eighth month, everything began to feel more real like what we had was no longer just new and exciting, but something deeper, something steady and meaningful. You slowly became a part of my everyday life in the most natural way, like my days felt incomplete without you in them. We shared everything random conversations, deep talks, small arguments, even comfortable silences and through it all, I could feel us growing closer. I started understanding you in ways I never thought I would, noticing the smallest things about you your moods, your thoughts, the way you care. It wasn't just about missing you anymore, it was about needing you, your presence, your voice, your energy. There were a few weeks that weren't easy, when we tried to stay away from each other, and it hurt more than I can explain but even in that distance, we didn't fall apart, we grew stronger. And that's what makes this even more real that despite everything, we found our way back to each other, and here we are today.

The Anniversary Phase

Next Few Months

And then came the last few month the ones that tested us in ways we never expected. We fought, we misunderstood each other, we tried to stay away, thinking maybe distance would make things easier but it never did. Instead, it only made us realize how much we truly meant to each other. There were nights filled with tears, moments of silence that felt heavier than words, and days where everything seemed uncertain. But even in all that pain, one thing never changed we never stopped caring, never stopped feeling, never truly let go. Somehow, through all the hurt and confusion, our hearts kept finding their way back to each other. And maybe that's what makes us real. not that we never broke, but that even after breaking, we chose each other again and again.

One Year

Us, Still

I know that you may never truly be mine in the way I've always imagined, I know that the life I dream of with you might never fully exist, and I understand the reality of everything we are facing. But even knowing all of that, I still choose you again and again because being with you, in whatever way I can, means everything to me. Every time I see you smile, it feels like I've achieved something meaningful in life. Every moment of peace we share makes me feel like the happiest person in the world. And I just wish that this feeling, this connection, never fades away.

Please never think that you aren't giving me enough because you are giving me more than I could ever ask for. Your time, your care, your emotions… they mean more to me than anything else ever could. You make me feel complete in a way I never thought possible. And all I truly want is to keep that beautiful smile on your face for as long as I can, to protect the peace we've built together, and to hold onto this for as many years as life allows us.
