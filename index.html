Our Story

The Beginning

The Day We Met

It still amazes me the way we met, two strangers on an app, unaware that one simple conversation would turn into something so deep and meaningful. Those first few months weren't just about knowing you, they were about feeling something I didn't even realize I was missing. Every message from you became the best part of my day, and I found myself waiting for you without even noticing it. Our late night conversations became my comfort, and somehow, despite the distance, you felt closer than anyone around me.

The Transitional Phase

First "I Miss You"

Somewhere between the fourth and eighth month, everything began to feel more real — like what we had was no longer just new and exciting, but something deeper, something steady and meaningful. You slowly became a part of my everyday life in the most natural way, like my days felt incomplete without you in them. We shared everything — random conversations, deep talks, small arguments, even comfortable silences — and through it all, I could feel us growing closer.

The Anniversary Phase

Next Few Months

And then came the last few months — the ones that tested us in ways we never expected. We fought, we misunderstood each other, we tried to stay away, thinking maybe distance would make things easier — but it never did. Instead, it only made us realize how much we truly meant to each other. Somehow, through all the hurt and confusion, our hearts kept finding their way back to each other.

One Year

Us, Still

I know that you may never truly be mine in the way I've always imagined. But even knowing all of that, I still choose you again and again — because being with you, in whatever way I can, means everything to me. Every time I see you smile, it feels like I've achieved something meaningful in life. And all I truly want is to keep that beautiful smile on your face for as long as I can.
